Volunteer Signup

Thank you for your interest in a volunteering with the Tiny Home Subcommittee. We're excited to work with you! We need help with the following:

  • helping out at the event - setting up, greeting attendees, clean up
  • getting the word out about the event - social media, expertise managing the media, etc
  • help with logistics leading up to the event
    • head up first aid (think through how much water we will need, sunscreen, first aid supplies)
    • head up setting up infrastructure (power cord, tables, chairs, tents)
